Generation of multiple aligned 3D objects

We evaluate our method in the generation of sets of aligned objects on 15 pairs of prompts describing pairs of objects with similar morphology but different geometry and appearance, such as a car and a carriage. We include various categories of objects, namely different kinds of animals, humanoids, plants, vehicles, furniture, and buildings.

Below, we show pairs of objects generated with existing methods and our method. Each row shows the results for one pair of prompts written below. For each method, we show one of the generated objects in the pair and the rendering of its geometry. Use the slider below to switch between the objects in the pair. Use the controls further down to switch between different examples.

Our method generates both objects in the pair simultaneously, while the other methods initially generate one of the object and then generate the other one from the first. We show two sets of results: with the object 1 generated first, denoted with p1→p2, and with the object 2 generated first, denoted with p1←p2.
